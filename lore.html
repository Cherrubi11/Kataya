Kataya was only thirteen the first time the curse took hold. A quiet village girl, lost to fire. She awoke beneath moonlight, gasping, untouched. No one remembered her but the stars.

In an age of steel and gods, Kataya became a sword-for-hire. Each life she led grew shorter, her memories heavier. She stopped using her real name. Until a child called her "star-eyed."
